Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30 • App: GridMaster Pro

GridMaster Pro ("we", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used and your rights regarding it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app is designed to work entirely on your device.

  • Local data: Your calculator inputs, appliance lists, language preference and theme are saved locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No accounts: No login, no email, no phone number is required.
  • No tracking: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracker.

2. Optional AI Assistant

The AI Assistant runs offline by default using a built-in knowledge base. If you choose to enable the optional online AI by entering your own OpenAI / Google Gemini API key:

  • Your questions are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you chose.
  • Your API key is stored only on your device (localStorage) and never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not see, log or store your questions or answers.
  • Please review the privacy policies of OpenAI and Google as applicable.

3. Permissions

GridMaster Pro may request the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — only needed to load the app initially and to use optional online AI. Removable from settings; the app works fully offline thereafter.
  • Camera — optional, only used by the AR Rooftop Solar Estimator feature when you tap "Open Camera". Images are processed locally on your device and never uploaded.
  • Storage — to save generated PDF reports to your device's Downloads folder.

4. Generated PDF Reports

Reports and quotations are generated and saved entirely on your device. We do not receive any copy of them.

5. Children's Privacy

GridMaster Pro is suitable for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Advertising (Free Version)

If a future free version includes ads (e.g., via Google AdMob), those ads may use a device advertising ID. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads from your Android Settings → Google → Ads. Pro/Paid version is ad-free.

7. Data Security

Because data stays on your device, security depends on your device's security. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your OS updated.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in the app's About → What's New section.

9. Contact

Questions? Email: support@gridmasterpro.app (replace with your real contact before publishing).

10. Your Rights

Under GDPR / DPDP (India) / similar laws, you have the right to access, correct or delete your data. Since we don't collect any data, you can simply clear app storage from Android Settings to remove all local data.
