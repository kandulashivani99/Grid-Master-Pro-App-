- [ ] Create new app in Play Console

## Pro Tips
1. **Internal Testing first** — test the actual .aab on a real device before public release.
2. **Keep your keystore safe** — losing it means you can never update the app.
3. **Start with a soft launch** in 1-2 countries to gather initial reviews.
4. **Respond to reviews** quickly — Google rewards active developers.
5. **Update regularly** — every 2-3 months bumps you up in search rankings.
